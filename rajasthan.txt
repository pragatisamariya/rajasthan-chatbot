RAJASTHAN COMPLETE TOURISM KNOWLEDGE BASE

STATE OVERVIEW
Rajasthan is the largest state of India by area.
Capital: Jaipur.
Nickname: Land of Kings.
Language: Hindi and Rajasthani dialects.
Currency: Indian Rupee (INR).
Climate: Mostly hot and dry with desert conditions.
Border: Shares international border with Pakistan.

HISTORICAL BACKGROUND
Rajasthan was ruled by Rajput kings known for bravery.
Important rulers include Maharana Pratap, Prithviraj Chauhan, Rana Sanga.
Architecture shows strong Rajput and Mughal influence.

MAJOR TOURIST CITIES

JAIPUR – Pink City
Known for pink architecture.
Attractions:
- Amber Fort
- Hawa Mahal
- City Palace
- Jantar Mantar (UNESCO site)
- Nahargarh Fort
Famous for shopping and handicrafts.

UDAIPUR – City of Lakes
Romantic city.
Attractions:
- Lake Pichola
- City Palace
- Sajjangarh Palace
- Jag Mandir
Popular for destination weddings and luxury hotels.

JAISALMER – Golden City
Located in Thar Desert.
Attractions:
- Jaisalmer Fort
- Sam Sand Dunes
- Patwon Ki Haveli
Activities: Camel safari, desert camping.

JODHPUR – Blue City
Famous for blue houses.
Attractions:
- Mehrangarh Fort
- Umaid Bhawan Palace
- Clock Tower Market.

AJMER
Ajmer Sharif Dargah is a major Sufi shrine.

PUSHKAR
Pushkar Lake and Brahma Temple.
Pushkar Camel Fair is globally famous.

RANTHAMBORE
Ranthambore National Park is famous for tiger safari.

GEOGRAPHY
Major desert: Thar Desert.
Mountain range: Aravalli Range.
Important river: Chambal River.

CLIMATE DETAILS
Summer: April–June (up to 48°C).
Monsoon: July–September.
Winter: October–March (Best time to visit).

FOOD
Dal Baati Churma.
Gatte ki Sabzi.
Ker Sangri.
Laal Maas.
Pyaaz Kachori.
Ghewar.

CULTURE
Folk dances: Ghoomar, Kalbeliya.
Major festivals:
- Desert Festival
- Teej Festival
- Gangaur Festival
- Pushkar Camel Fair
- Mewar Festival
Traditional dress:
Women wear Ghagra Choli.
Men wear Dhoti Kurta with colorful turban.

WILDLIFE
Tigers: Ranthambore.
Leopards: Jawai.
Camels: Desert region.
Other parks:
- Sariska Tiger Reserve
- Desert National Park
- Keoladeo National Park (Bird Sanctuary)

DISTANCE BETWEEN CITIES
Jaipur to Udaipur: 400 km.
Jaipur to Jodhpur: 350 km.
Jodhpur to Jaisalmer: 280 km.
Udaipur to Jodhpur: 250 km.

TRAVEL TIPS
Best time: October to March.
Carry sunscreen in summer.
Winters are cold at night.
Bargain in local markets.
Book safari in advance.

BUDGET ESTIMATE
Budget trip: ₹1000–₹2000 per day.
Mid-range: ₹3000–₹6000 per day.
Luxury: ₹10,000+ per day.

Yes, generally safe.

Yes, especially Udaipur.

7–10 days recommended.
